#DESCRIPTION: Horses (Equus ferus caballus) are large, herbivorous, four-legged mammals with a single-toed, hoofed foot, known for their strength, speed, and long association with humans. They have long, muscular necks, long heads, and mane hair, with heights typically around 1.5 meters (5 feet) at the shoulder. They are social, intelligent, and highly sensitive.
